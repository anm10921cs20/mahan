    • Mr. R.KIRUBAKARAN

    Chairman

    Email: chairman@tvrasc.in

    It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Thiruvalluvar Arts & Science College, a distinguished institution founded by Shree Jayaganapathy Educational Trust in 1997. As one of the oldest colleges in Cuddalore District, we take pride in our tradition of providing quality higher education.

    Our college thrives on the collective efforts of our management, faculty, staff, and students, all working together to uphold our values and culture. We are committed to nurturing students not just within the classroom but beyond, preparing them to become skilled professionals ready to excel in their chosen fields.

    With a strong foundation in Management, Commerce, Language, Literature, and the Sciences, including UG and PG programs in Computer Science, our alumni have gone on to achieve success both in India and abroad. Our mission is to achieve autonomous status and continue serving the community, especially the underprivileged in and around Kurinjipadi





    About Us

    Thiruvalluvar Arts and Science College, established by Shri Jayaganapathy Educational Trust in 1996, was founded with the mission to provide quality education in Kurinjipadi, a region historically underrepresented in academic development within the Cuddalore District.
